Privacy Policy

StepLock does not collect, store, or share personal data.

Permissions

StepLock requires the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Activity Recognition — used to count steps while an alarm is ringing. Step data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted.
  • Notifications — used to display alarm notifications and full-screen alerts.
  • Exact Alarms — required to ensure alarms ring at the correct time.

StepLock does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers.
